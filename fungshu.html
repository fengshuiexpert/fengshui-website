What is Fung Shu (Feng Shui)?

Literally it means wind and water. It is the ancient Chinese art of positioning buildings and furniture to create a harmonious flow of Chi (energy) to support our well being (promote health, wealth and happiness).

What takes place during a Fung Shu (Feng Shui) consultation?

In Frances's own words:

“I come to my clients' homes, offices or shops to conduct a consultation. It involves the following steps:

  1. “I have my clients clarify their objectives for the consultation. For example, the most common objectives are creating harmonious energy, attracting abundance in life, cultivating healthy relationships and careers.
  2. “Then I Feng Shui the energy outside of the home. I see if the neighborhood buildings, roads or objects create negative energy towards the house or office.
  3. “I move on to Feng Shui the interior of the house or office, keeping the client's objectives in mind so that I don't have to work on every Feng Shui issue in the house.
  4. “During the consultation, I educate my clients on how to identify good Chi and bad Chi, and I work with them to find personalized Feng Shui remedies that resonate with their energy to balance the undesirable Chi inside and outside the house.
  5. “At the end I give follow-up instructions to my clients.”
