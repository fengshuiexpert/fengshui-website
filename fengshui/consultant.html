Frances He, Ph.D.

Dr. Frances He is one of the top Feng Shui practitioners in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Trained in Classical Chinese Feng Shui

Frances was born and educated in Canton, China. She inherits her skills from a long lineage of traditional Chinese Feng Shui masters. Her training began at the age of five when her grandfather decided it was time to introduce her to the art of Feng Shui. What he imparted to her was a wisdom and knowledge that takes a lifetime to master.

After emigrating to the United States as a youth, she honored her grandfather's wishes and studied Feng Shui with a number of Chinese teachers resident or traveling to the U.S., most notably the esteemed Professor Lin Yun. As a bicultural expert, fluent in English as well as Cantonese and Mandarin, Dr. He is uniquely able to translate the concepts and analysis of classical Chinese Feng Shui for American clients.

U.S. Doctorate in Psychology

Frances has been fortunate to receive the full benefit of a U.S. education to complement the knowledge of her own heritage. She received her B.A degree from U.C. Berkeley (in Computer Science) and then entered graduate school in psychology. Fulfilling her personal dream, she obtained her Ph.D. in psychology from John F. Kennedy University. She maintains a busy practice in San Francisco and Berkeley as a licensed psychotherapist. Her Feng Shui practice is a way to offer the knowledge of her ancestors in a form that is popular among American clients.

Feng Shui Consulting

Dr. He provides Feng Shui consulting for clients' residences, retail shops, and business offices. She has developed a specialty practice for clients in Chiropractic and Law. She is available for onsite appointments throughout the San Francisco Bay Area.

Feng Shui Teaching

Dr. He has honored the legacy of her ancestors by introducing Feng Shui to students through classes, as well as engaging in outreach to other professions and the general public. Among other engagements, Dr. He has been honored to address the faculty at U.C. San Francisco and designers at American Institute of Architects (AIA).
